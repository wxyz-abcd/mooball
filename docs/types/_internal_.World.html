    kickBall

    • kickBall(pDisc: MovableDisc, bDisc: MovableDisc, pp: PlayerPhysics): boolean

      Creates the physical ball kicking effect. No kicking will happen if the distance between ball and player is >= 4 map units.

      Parameters

      • pDisc: MovableDisc

        The actual disc of the player that is kicking the ball.

      • bDisc: MovableDisc

        The actual disc of the ball that is being kicked.

      • pp: PlayerPhysics

        The current physical attributes of the players.

      Returns boolean

      Whether the ball was kicked or not.
